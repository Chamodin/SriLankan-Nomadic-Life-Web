Colombo


Explore the vibrant city of Colombo, Sri Lanka, where a rich tapestry of culture, history, and modernity awaits. Discover a myriad of tourist attractions that showcase the essence of this bustling metropolis. Discover the diverse facets of Colombo, where ancient traditions harmonize with the pulse of contemporary life. Whether you're a history buff, a nature lover, or a shopaholic, Colombo offers an array of experiences to captivate every traveler.


Gangaramaya Temple

Gangaramaya is one of the oldest Buddhist temples in Colombo, started by the famous scholar monk Hikkaduwe Sri Sumangala Nayaka Thera in the late 19th Century.

Galle Face

Galle Face is a 5 ha (12 acres) ocean-side urban park, which stretches for 500 m (1,600 ft) along the coast, in the heart of Colombo.

Map

Independence Square

Independence Memorial Hall is a national monument in Sri Lanka built for commemoration of the independence of Sri Lanka from the British rule with the restoration of full governing responsibility to a Ceylonese-elected legislature on 4 February 1948

Jami Ul -Alfar Mosque

Landmark mosque constructed in 1908, known for its distinctive red & white patterned facade.

Port City
